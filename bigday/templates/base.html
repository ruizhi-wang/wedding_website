Travel and Accomodation

How to get there

The only thing you need to know is how to get to the church on the day of wedding. After that, it is enough just to follow the crowd. However, Veauche is a small town, with limited access to public transport.

Eglise de Veauche Bourg
17 place Abbé Blard
42340 Veauche

The closest airport to Veauche is in Lyon. Upon arrival, the easiest way is probably to rent a car and to drive to Veauche. There is plenty of place to park at the church and afterwards at the reception. But if you prefer to take public transport to protect the environment (you won't be protecting your wallet though), we have added some instructions below. You can check the train times and book tickets on the website of the national railway company SNCF ( link to website ).

  • Lyon Airport to Lyon Train Station (Lyon Part-Dieu)

    Take the Rhone Express from the airport to Lyon Part-Dieu, which is the main train station. The trip takes about 30 minutes and costs €17 one-way per person. Be aware that it only runs until midnight!

  • Lyon Station (Lyon Part-Dieu) to Veauche Station (Veauche St. Galmier)

    Take the train and you will need to change at St. Etienne Chateaucreux. The trip takes roughly 1.5 hours and costs between €10 - €15 one-way per person. The connection only runs every couple of hours.

  • Veauche Station (Veauche St. Galmier) to Church

    That's the easy part. Let us know a bit in advance and we will find some relatives to do us the favour of picking you up!
